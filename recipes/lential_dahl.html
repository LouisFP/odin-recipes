Lentil Dahl

Description

A classic dish full of protein from the split red lentils which is served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • White onion
  • Sunflower oil
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Chillis
  • Ginger
  • Turmeric
  • Sweet potato (or indeed any addition to the dahl, carrots or cauliflower make a great addition)
  • Red split lentils

Instructions

  1. Create the basic sauce by frying the onions and adding in the cumin, coriander, ginger, turmeric, and chilli.
  2. Blend to taste.
  3. Parboil the sweet potatoes (or vegetable of your choice)
  4. Add lentils and water
  5. Allow to simmer
  6. Et, voilá!
